// What is Miden

Practical privacy
for the future of finance.

Miden combines high throughput, private transactions, and verifiable computation — designed to power the next generation of financial applications that demand both privacy and compliance.

01
Customizable Privacy
Miden gives builders the choice between public and private transactions, enabling a new class of business applications that demand both compliance and confidentiality.
02
Quantum Secure
Built with post-quantum cryptography, Miden is designed to future-proof your business on-chain — ready for what comes after today's cryptographic standards.
03
Regulation Ready
With programmable privacy at its core, Miden is built for a future of regulatory clarity — enabling selective disclosure, compliance proofs, and auditable privacy flows.

// The Team

The builders behind Miden.

Engineers, cryptographers, and product thinkers — former Meta/Facebook blockchain researchers focused on turning advanced ZK research into real financial infrastructure.

Co-Founder & CTO
Bobbin Threadbare
Former lead ZK researcher at Meta/Facebook's Novi, where he built Winterfell — one of the fastest STARK provers ever written. Creator of the Miden VM and the Distaff VM. Pioneer of the edge blockchain paradigm, and first to release an open-source STARK prover (genSTARK). Widely regarded as one of the foremost STARK experts in the world.
@bobbinth
Co-Founder & Head of Product
Dominik Schmid
Former engineer at ConsenSys and the Raiden Network. Leads product and protocol design at Miden, responsible for the account-and-note model at the heart of Miden's privacy architecture. Architect of Miden's tiered sparse Merkle tree and its approach to parallel, private transaction execution.
@schmiddominik1
Co-Founder
Azeem Khan
Former Gitcoin fundraising lead and ex-ConsenSys. Co-founder of MorphL2. Consultant to the UNICEF CryptoFund, WEF Future of Global FinTech contributor, Forbes Crypto columnist, and CoinDesk columnist. Named to the Faces of Crypto Philanthropy list in 2023 & 2024 and 100 Faces of Crypto by Stand With Crypto.
@azeemk_
